About Me

Hello, my name is Vlad Shapochnikov. I am a freelance front-end developer, designer and a full-time adventurer. I enjoy designing thoughtful, human-centered products and experiences. I see the world as a place to learn and I'm curious about the people around me. I particularly enjoy travelling, photography, and I love Chipotle.
